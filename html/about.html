About the Game

The goal of this game is to take the money you are given and to buy food for one week. After purchasing food, you return home and you ration your food so that you get the most nutrition while not wasting any food. You are awarded points for how much the nutrition (red, yellow, and green) bars are filled. Food that is not eaten or that expires will deduct from your final score.
